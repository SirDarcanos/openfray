OpenFray

OpenFray is a free, browser-based combat console for running DnD 5e encounters. It tracks initiative, monster resources, conditions, concentration, group saves, and honest dice in one fast, glanceable place — built for Dungeon Masters who need to keep hold of what's happening in a fight without losing the thread.

Open the console →

The state others forget

Who has advantage on whom, who's debuffed, who's concentrating — the between-creature state other trackers drop on the floor.

Monster resources, handled

Recharge abilities, legendary actions, Legendary Resistance, lair actions, concentration, and reactions — all tracked as you play.

One-tap group saves

Roll a whole pack's save against a single effect at once, with resistances, immunities, and Evasion applied for you.

Honest, transparent dice

Cryptographically random rolls with a clear, effect-aware log. No fudging, no “feels-fair” tampering — ever.

Build your own

Custom creatures and spells with a full stat-block editor, a reusable characters roster, and campaigns with house rules.

Free, and yours

Runs fully in your browser with no account. Sign in free with Discord or Google to save and sync across devices.
