OpenFray

A free, open-source DnD 5e combat tracker for Dungeon Masters. Fast, glanceable, and focused on the one job other trackers do badly: keeping hold of what's actually happening in a fight.

  • Monster resource tracking
  • Relational effects & reminders
  • One-tap mass saves
  • Honest, transparent dice
  • SRD compendium
